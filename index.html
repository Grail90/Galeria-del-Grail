This is Our Story

Our dream to build a safe space for all Art Lovers

Founded and Established in 2012, the sole purpose of Grail Gallery has always been to help and Support Artists around the world. We help them sell their Art and give them a platform to market themselves and connect with other Artists. It has been the stepping stone alot of Upcoming Artist started from and a support for already established Artists. Grail Gallery is also a great place for Art lovers to search and get quality works from local and International artists or just sight-see If that is what they are after.
